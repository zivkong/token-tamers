# Achievements

Achievements are Token Tamers' record of everything your work has accomplished — every
first, every milestone, every quiet week that paid off. There are **44** of them, and
none ask anything of you beyond coding the way you already do. Many hand back a
**reward**: a [trinket](trinkets.md), a [habitat](unlockables.md), or a **title**.

Achievements are one of the four pillars of the **completionist North Star** (100% =
Dex + achievements + habitats + trinkets). They carry serious weight in the overall
completion meter — collecting them is most of the long game.

> Nothing here is permanently missable. Even the pattern-locked and weather-style
> unlocks are re-earnable; a milestone you haven't hit is simply still ahead.
