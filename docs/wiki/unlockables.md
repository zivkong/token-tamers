# Unlockables

Everything your pet can earn that isn't a species or a stat: the **habitats** it lives
in, the **titles** you carry, and the **trinkets** you decorate with. All of it is
cosmetic — unlockables celebrate how you play, they never change power.

Together they make up the **completionist North Star**: 100% completion is the Dex plus
**achievements**, **habitats**, and **trinkets**, and the completion meter weights them
**40 / 40 / 10 / 10** (Dex and achievements carry the most, habitats and trinkets round
it out). Nothing here is ever permanently missable.

## Habitats

A **habitat** is the scene your pet lives in — its backdrop, palette, and a set of
**trinket slots** to decorate. You start with the **Terminal Den** and earn the rest
through [achievements](achievements.md). Switch between any you've unlocked from
Settings.

| Habitat           | How you unlock it                       | Achievement            |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Terminal Den**  | Starting habitat — yours from `tt init` | _(default)_            |
| **Rooftop Night** | Lock the **Vigil** pattern              | _Watcher of the Night_ |
| **Meadow**        | Lock the **Prism** pattern              | _Spectrum Complete_    |
| **Beach Cove**    | Earn a vacation spot                    | _Shore Leave_          |
| **Forest Glade**  | Earn a quiet glade                      | _Into the Green_       |
| **Snowpeak**      | Grow strong enough for the high peaks   | _Summit Reached_       |
| **Desert Dunes**  | Endure five generations                 | _Into the Dusk_        |
| **Crimson Mars**  | Reach **Grade A**                       | _Red Horizon_          |
| **Europa Ice**    | Fill **10%** of the Dex                 | _Ice Moon_             |
| **Lunar Base**    | Reach the **7th** generation            | _Moonwalker_           |
| **Nebula Drift**  | Fill **25%** of the Dex                 | _Between the Stars_    |
| **Starship Deck** | Reach the **10th** generation           | _Warp Speed_           |

Twelve in all: a homely Terminal Den, four earthly scenes, and a climb through the
solar system as your lineage deepens.

## Titles

A **title** is an honorific your achievements grant you — a quiet badge of what your
tamer has done. Eleven exist:

| Title                | Earned with                                     | Achievement          |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| **Apex Tamer**       | Reach the **Apex** stage                        | _Apex Achieved_      |
| **Lineage Keeper**   | Reach the **5th** generation                    | _Lineage Begins_     |
| **Ancient Tamer**    | Reach the **10th** generation                   | _Ancient Line_       |
| **Survivor**         | Endure a week of **Dormancy**                   | _Through the Cocoon_ |
| **Master of Change** | Accumulate **50 molts** across all lifetimes    | _Master of Change_   |
| **Collector**        | Fill **50%** of the Dex                         | _Halfway There_      |
| **Aether Master**    | Reach Apex with a full **Aether** lineage       | _Aether Mastery_     |
| **Cipher Master**    | Reach Apex with a full **Cipher** lineage       | _Cipher Mastery_     |
| **Flux Master**      | Reach Apex with a full **Flux** lineage         | _Flux Mastery_       |
| **Forge Master**     | Reach Apex with a full **Forge** lineage        | _Forge Mastery_      |
| **Bloom Master**     | Reach Apex with a full **Wild (Bloom)** lineage | _Bloom Mastery_      |
